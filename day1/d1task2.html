Albert Einstein

Early Life

Albert Einstein was born on March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany. He showed an early interest in mathematics and science, often questioning the nature of the world around him.

Education

Einstein attended the Polytechnic Institute in Zurich, where he studied physics and mathematics. After graduating in 1900, he struggled to find a teaching position.

Career

In 1905, Einstein published four groundbreaking papers that revolutionized the understanding of physics. His theory of relativity changed the way scientists viewed space, time, and gravity.
