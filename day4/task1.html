About Cats

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that are often kept as pets. They are known for their agility, independence, and playful behavior. Domestic cats belong to the Felidae family and have a wide variety of breeds, each with unique traits.

They have been companions to humans for thousands of years, valued for both their hunting abilities and affectionate behavior.
